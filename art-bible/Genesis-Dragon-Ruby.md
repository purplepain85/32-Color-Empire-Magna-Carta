# RUBY GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Hovering Heartfire
### Color: Ruby (#E0115F)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Hovering Heartfire  
Category: Aerial  
Description:
- Dragon suspended mid-air in a stable hover.
- Wings beating in a slow, powerful rhythm.
- Tail curled into a perfect spiral beneath the body.
- Chest pushed forward, glowing with internal heat.
- Head angled slightly downward in a calm, confident gaze.
- Silhouette expresses warmth, presence, and emotional intensity.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Ruby (#E0115F)
- Secondary Scales: Deep wine-red gradients along spine and wing bones
- Belly Scales: Soft rose-gold glow, pulsing subtly
- Wing Membranes: Translucent ruby with bright inner highlights
- Horns & Claws: Polished garnet with reflective surfaces
- Eyes: Warm gold with a soft inner radiance

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Soft upward glow from the chest area.
- Gentle rim-light in rose-gold along wing edges.
- Subsurface scattering in wing membranes.
- No harsh shadows — lighting should feel warm and emotional.
- Slight aura effect around the chest to emphasize “heartfire.”

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: High-altitude sky with soft pink and red clouds  
Foreground: Floating embers drifting upward  
Palette: Rose, ruby, soft gold, warm twilight tones

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s third spark.
- Embodies warmth, emotional power, and inner fire.
- The dragon of compassion, passion, and radiant presence.
- Where Crimson is ignition and Scarlet is direction, Ruby is emotion and heart.
