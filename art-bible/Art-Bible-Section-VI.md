# ART BIBLE v1.0  
## Section VI — The 32‑Color Pantheon  
*(Definitions, Emotional Signatures, Metaphysical Roles)*

---

## VI.1 — Purpose of the Pantheon
The 32‑Color Pantheon is the metaphysical backbone of the Empire.  
Each color represents:

- A cosmic principle  
- An emotional signature  
- A governance archetype  
- A visual identity  
- A symbolic geometry  

These 32 colors form the Empire’s spiritual, artistic, and constitutional foundation.

---

## VI.2 — Structure of the Pantheon
The Pantheon is divided into **four Courts**, each containing **eight colors**:

### **1. The Court of Light**  
Colors tied to clarity, perception, and ascension.

### **2. The Court of Flame**  
Colors tied to will, passion, and transformation.

### **3. The Court of Earth**  
Colors tied to stability, growth, and renewal.

### **4. The Court of Shadow**  
Colors tied to judgment, memory, and the unseen.

Each Court has its own emotional tone and symbolic geometry.

## VI.4 — Color Archetype Format
Each color in the Pantheon follows a strict identity structure:

- **Emotional Signature**  
- **Metaphysical Role**  
- **Visual Expression**  
- **Symbolic Geometry**  
- **Chain Variants (Solana vs Polygon)**  

This ensures consistency across all dragons, artifacts, and future expansions.

---

## VI.5 — Example Archetype Breakdown

### **Amber — Memory**
- **Emotional:** Nostalgia, remembrance  
- **Metaphysical:** Keeper of the past  
- **Visual:** Warm gold‑orange gradients, soft glow  
- **Symbolic:** Circular spirals, echo motifs  
- **Solana Variant:** Fluid, luminous transitions  
- **Polygon Variant:** Structured, crystalline reflections
