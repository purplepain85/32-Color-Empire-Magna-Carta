# SAPPHIRE GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Cloud-Cutter Turn
### Color: Sapphire (#0F52BA)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Cloud-Cutter Turn  
Category: Aerial  
Description:
- Dragon banking sharply through dense clouds.
- One wing dipped low, the other raised high in a tight turning arc.
- Tail sweeping outward to counterbalance the maneuver.
- Neck angled forward, eyes locked on a target beyond the frame.
- Silhouette expresses precision flight, mastery of the skies, and fluid motion.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Sapphire (#0F52BA)
- Secondary Scales: Deep ocean-blue gradients along spine and wing bones
- Belly Scales: Cool silver-blue with faint reflective sheen
- Wing Membranes: Translucent blue with soft inner glow
- Horns & Claws: Polished steel-gray with subtle blue reflections
- Eyes: Bright icy blue with a sharp, focused gaze

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Strong side-lighting to emphasize the banking motion.
- Cool highlights along the upper wing and spine.
- Soft diffused light from surrounding clouds.
- Mist and cloud particles catching light around the dragon.
- No warm tones — lighting must remain cold, crisp, and atmospheric.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: High-altitude storm clouds with pockets of blue sky  
Foreground: Cloud wisps trailing from wing edges  
Palette: Deep blue, steel gray, cold white, atmospheric haze

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s fourth spark.
- Embodies clarity, intellect, and mastery of perspective.
- The dragon of insight, strategy, and aerial dominance.
- Where Ruby is emotion, Sapphire is thought — cool, sharp, and precise.
