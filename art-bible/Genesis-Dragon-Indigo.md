# INDIGO GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Deep Horizon
### Color: Indigo (#4B0082)

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Deep Horizon  
Category: Majestic / Ascendant  
Description:
- Dragon standing on a rocky outcrop with wings partially raised, as if preparing for a long, deliberate ascent.
- Body angled forward, posture strong and grounded.
- Tail extends behind in a slow, powerful curve.
- Head lifted toward the distance, eyes focused on an unseen horizon.
- Silhouette evokes depth, mystery, and the pull of the unknown.

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Low, directional lighting from behind or below to emphasize depth.
- Soft rim‑light along wings and horns to carve out the silhouette.
- Occasional drifting particles resembling distant starlight.
- Highlights should feel cosmic, quiet, and vast.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Cliffside overlooking a deep twilight sky  
Foreground: Wind‑swept stone, faint drifting dust  
Palette: Indigo, midnight blue, deep violet, soft silver

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s twenty‑fifth spark.
- Embodies depth, intuition, and the pull toward the unknown.
- The dragon of horizons, mysteries, and the unseen layers of reality.
- Where Lavender drifts through dreams, Indigo gazes into the infinite — calm, vast, and profoundly aware.
