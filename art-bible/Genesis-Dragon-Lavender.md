### Pose: The Whispered Dream

### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Whispered Dream  
Category: Floating / Ethereal  
Description:
- Dragon suspended in a gentle mid‑air drift, body relaxed and weightless.
- Wings unfurled in a soft, curved arc, like petals caught in a breeze.
- Tail trails behind in a slow, ribbon‑like flow.
- Head tilted slightly upward, eyes half‑closed in a dreamy, tranquil expression.
- Silhouette evokes softness, imagination, and drifting thought.

- Primary Scales: Lavender (#C8A2C8)

- Cool, diffused lighting with a gentle violet tint.
- Soft bloom around the wings and tail.
- Subtle drifting particles resembling dream‑dust or floating spores.
- Highlights should feel airy, weightless, and slightly surreal.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Twilight meadow, dreamscape glade, or floating lavender mist  
Foreground: Drifting petals, soft haze, faint sparkles  
Palette: Lavender, lilac, periwinkle, pale silver

- Represents the Empire’s twenty‑fourth spark.
- Embodies imagination, intuition, and the quiet magic of the subconscious.
- The dragon of dreams, creativity, and inner worlds.
- Where Rose blooms with emotion, Lavender drifts through thought — soft, mystical, and endlessly imaginative.
