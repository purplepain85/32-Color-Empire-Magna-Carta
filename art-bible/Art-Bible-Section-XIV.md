# ART BIBLE v1.0  
## Section XIV — Genesis Sigil Page Rules  
*(The Eight‑Sided Sphere, Color Display, Title Emblazoning, Reveal Logic)*

---

## XIV.1 — Purpose of the Genesis Sigil Page
The Genesis Sigil Page is the **first page** of every Genesis Dragon’s presentation.  
It serves as:

- A ceremonial introduction  
- A color identity declaration  
- A premium reveal mechanic  
- A unifying visual standard  
- A collectible‑grade signature  

The Sigil Page is the Empire’s equivalent of a royal crest.

---

## XIV.2 — Core Components of the Sigil Page
Every Sigil Page contains exactly **three** elements:

1. **The Eight‑Sided Gem‑Sphere (The Sigil)**  
2. **The Color Identity**  
3. **The Emblazoned Title Across the Top**

No additional elements may be added.

---

## XIV.3 — The Eight‑Sided Gem‑Sphere
The Sigil is the sacred geometric object representing the dragon’s essence.

### Required characteristics:
- Perfect eight‑sided spherical geometry  
- Smooth, mythic curvature  
- Subtle internal glow  
- Color‑pure surface  
- No textures, cracks, or imperfections  
- No symbols inside the sphere  

### Forbidden:
- Faceted gemstone cuts  
- Sci‑fi holograms  
- Metallic reflections  
- Cartoon shine  
- Overly bright neon glow  

The Sigil must feel ancient, cosmic, and ceremonial.

---

## XIV.4 — Color Display Rules
The Sigil must display **only the dragon’s core color**, expressed through:

- Pure hue  
- Controlled highlights  
- Deepened shadows  
- Subtle atmospheric glow  

No gradients that introduce unrelated colors.

### Chain variants:
- **Solana:** Soft, fluid glow  
- **Polygon:** Structured, crystalline light behavior  

Both must remain subtle and elegant.

---

## XIV.5 — Emblazoned Title Rules
The title across the top must follow these rules:

- Centered  
- All caps  
- Serif or runic‑inspired typeface  
- No modern fonts  
- No neon or metallic effects  
- No drop shadows
