# EMERALD GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Verdant Sentinel
### Color: Emerald (#50C878)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Verdant Sentinel  
Category: Perched  
Description:
- Dragon perched on a cliff edge, front-facing and regal.
- Wings partially folded, forming a protective arc around the body.
- Tail draped downward with a gentle curve.
- Neck tall and steady, head held with calm authority.
- Silhouette expresses vigilance, balance, and natural sovereignty.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Emerald (#50C878)
- Secondary Scales: Deep forest-green gradients along spine and limbs
- Belly Scales: Soft moss-green with subtle reflective sheen
- Wing Membranes: Translucent green with faint leaf-like veining
- Horns & Claws: Polished dark wood-brown with natural texture
- Eyes: Bright verdant green with a serene, grounded gaze

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Soft natural lighting from above and slightly behind.
- Gentle green bounce-light from surrounding foliage.
- Subtle highlights along wing edges and horns.
- No harsh shadows — lighting should feel organic and calm.
- Mist or pollen particles catching light around the dragon.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Lush cliffside overlooking a vast emerald forest  
Foreground: Moss, vines, drifting pollen motes  
Palette: Forest green, moss green, soft gold, natural earth tones

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s fifth spark.
- Embodies growth, stability, and natural harmony.
- The dragon of balance, stewardship, and grounded wisdom.
- Where Sapphire is intellect, Emerald is life — steady, patient, and enduring.
