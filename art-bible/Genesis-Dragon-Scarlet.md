# SCARLET GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Diving Spear
### Color: Scarlet (#FF2400)

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Diving Spear  
Category: Aerial  
Description:
- Dragon in a steep downward dive, body streamlined like a spear.
- Wings swept sharply backward to reduce drag.
- Tail extended straight behind in a perfect aerodynamic line.
- Neck aligned with the spine for maximum velocity.
- Claws tucked inward, silhouette forming a V-shaped spearhead.
- Represents precision, aggression, and decisive action.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Scarlet (#FF2400)

- Belly Scales: Pale molten gold for contrast

- Horns & Claws: Polished obsidian, sharp and angular
- Eyes: Bright gold with a razor-thin vertical pupil

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Strong top-down lighting to emphasize the dive.
- Hard highlights along spine and wing edges.
- Subtle heat streaks trailing behind wings.
- Motion blur applied only to background elements.
- No ambient or soft lighting — directional and sharp only.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Storm-lit sky with red sunlight breaking through clouds  
Foreground: Wind streaks, scattered embers trailing behind  
Palette: Deep storm gray, scarlet light shafts, orange-gold atmospheric glow

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s second spark.
- Embodies focus, intensity, and forward momentum.
- The dragon of decisive action and precision strikes.
- Where Crimson is ignition, Scarlet is direction and intent.
