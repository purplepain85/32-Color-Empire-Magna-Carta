# ART BIBLE v1.0  
## Section XV — The Genesis Dragon Page Layout  
*(Final Presentation, Hierarchy, Spacing, Composition Rules)*

---

## XV.1 — Purpose of the Dragon Page Layout
The Genesis Dragon Page is the **primary collectible display** for each dragon.  
It must:

- Present the dragon with mythic clarity  
- Maintain consistent hierarchy  
- Reinforce color and chain identity  
- Provide a premium, ceremonial experience  
- Match the Sigil Page reveal flow  

This is the Empire’s “master portrait” standard.

---

## XV.2 — Page Structure Overview
Every Genesis Dragon Page contains **four** required components:

1. **The Dragon Artwork (centerpiece)**  
2. **The Title Bar**  
3. **The Color Identity Tag**  
4. **The Chain Identity Tag**  

No additional components may be added.

---

## XV.3 — Dragon Artwork Rules
The artwork must:

- Occupy 60–75% of the page  
- Maintain a clean silhouette  
- Avoid clipping or cropping important anatomy  
- Use approved lighting, materials, and background rules  
- Match the dragon’s metadata exactly  

The dragon is the unquestioned focal point.

---

## XV.4 — Title Bar Rules
