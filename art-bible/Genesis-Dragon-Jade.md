# JADE GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Serpent’s Spiral
### Color: Jade (#00A86B)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Serpent’s Spiral  
Category: Grounded / Coiled  
Description:
- Dragon coiled in a tall vertical spiral, body rising like a living helix.
- Wings folded tightly to emphasize the serpentine form.
- Tail anchored at the base, providing stability for the upward coil.
- Head elevated at the top of the spiral, eyes calm and calculating.
- Silhouette expresses ancient wisdom, fluid motion, and disciplined control.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Jade (#00A86B)
- Secondary Scales: Deep forest-teal gradients along spine and ridges
- Belly Scales: Pale mint-green with soft reflective sheen
- Wing Membranes: Translucent jade with faint marbling patterns
- Horns & Claws: Polished dark stone with green mineral veins
- Eyes: Bright emerald with a slow, deliberate glow

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Soft diffused lighting from above, like filtered forest light.
- Gentle green bounce-light from surrounding foliage.
- Highlights along the spiral curve to emphasize the helix shape.
- No harsh shadows — lighting should feel ancient, natural, and meditative.
- Occasional drifting pollen or dust catching the light.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Dense ancient forest with shafts of filtered sunlight  
Foreground: Moss, vines, drifting pollen motes  
Palette: Jade, deep green, soft gold, forest teal

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s fourteenth spark.
- Embodies ancient wisdom, patience, and disciplined growth.
- The dragon of meditation, memory, and the slow mastery of time.
- Where Cobalt is relentless momentum, Jade is deliberate evolution — calm, coiled, and eternal.
