# OBSIDIAN GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Abyssal Ascend
### Color: Obsidian (#0B0B0B)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Abyssal Ascend  
Category: Aerial / Shadow  
Description:
- Dragon rising vertically from darkness, wings unfurling in a sharp, angular spread.
- Body emerging from below, as if rising from a void or deep chasm.
- Tail trailing downward into shadow, fading into blackness.
- Head tilted slightly downward, eyes glowing like twin embers in the dark.
- Silhouette expresses dread, power, and the emergence of something ancient and unstoppable.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Obsidian (#0B0B0B)
- Secondary Scales: Deep graphite gradients along spine and wing ridges
- Belly Scales: Matte black with almost no reflectivity
- Wing Membranes: Near-black with faint glass-like sheen at the edges
- Horns & Claws: Razor-sharp volcanic glass with reflective facets
- Eyes: Burning ember-red or molten orange, piercing through darkness

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Ultra-low lighting from below, creating dramatic upward shadows.
- Sharp rim-light along wings and horns to define silhouette.
- Subtle reflective glints on obsidian surfaces.
- Ambient light should be nearly nonexistent.
- Occasional drifting ash or dust illuminated by faint light.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Deep volcanic chasm or abyssal void  
Foreground: Rising ash, drifting soot, faint ember sparks  
Palette: Black, charcoal, ember-red, deep volcanic tones

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s seventeenth spark.
- Embodies darkness, rebirth, and the power that rises from the depths.
- The dragon of shadow-forging, hidden strength, and ancient memory.
- Where Amethyst is cosmic stillness, Obsidian is primordial emergence — sharp, silent, and forged in the deep.
