## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Tidal Flow  
Category: Fluid / Dynamic  
Description:
- Dragon in a sweeping, wave‑like motion, body curved in an elegant S‑shape.
- Wings partially extended, membranes flowing like water caught mid‑surge.
- Tail arcs forward in a smooth, ribbon‑like sweep, echoing the motion of tides.
- Head angled slightly downward, eyes calm and observant.
- Silhouette evokes flowing water, ocean currents, and effortless motion.

- Primary Scales: Teal (#008080)
- Secondary Scales: Sea‑foam gradients of turquoise and deep aqua along spine and limbs

- Wing Membranes: Translucent teal with ripple‑like patterns and shifting highlights

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Cool, diffused lighting with soft blue‑green reflections.
- Highlights should mimic water glints — sharp but gentle.
- Subtle drifting mist or spray around the dragon’s lower body.
- Occasional refracted light patterns, like sunlight through shallow water.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Coastal cliff, tidal pool, or shimmering shoreline  
Foreground: Mist, water spray, faint ripple effects  
Palette: Teal, aqua, turquoise, pale sea‑mist

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s twenty‑eighth spark.
- Embodies adaptability, flow, and the calm strength of moving water.
- The dragon of tides, transitions, and emotional equilibrium.
- Where Navy watches from the depths, Teal moves with the currents — fluid, balanced, and ever‑shifting.
