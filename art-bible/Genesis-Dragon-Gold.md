# GOLD GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Treasure Grip
### Color: Gold (#FFD700)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Treasure Grip  
Category: Perched  
Description:
- Dragon perched firmly atop a stone pedestal or ancient plinth.
- One foreclaw gripping a glowing golden stone or relic.
- Wings partially unfurled in a proud, expansive arc.
- Chest lifted, posture confident and commanding.
- Silhouette expresses wealth, authority, and radiant dominance.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Gold (#FFD700)
- Secondary Scales: Deep metallic amber along spine and wing bones
- Belly Scales: Soft pale-gold with high reflectivity
- Wing Membranes: Translucent gold with shimmering metallic highlights
- Horns & Claws: Polished metallic gold with sharp, faceted edges
- Eyes: Bright molten gold with a steady, regal glow

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Strong warm key light from the upper right.
- High reflectivity on scales — bright specular highlights.
- Subtle glints on horns, claws, and the held relic.
- Soft ambient bounce-light from surrounding stone.
- Occasional sparkle effects on the brightest surfaces.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Ancient sunlit ruins or a golden temple terrace  
Foreground: Dust motes drifting through warm light  
Palette: Gold, amber, sandstone, soft white sunbeams

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s ninth spark.
- Embodies prosperity, achievement, and radiant power.
- The dragon of legacy, earned wealth, and sovereign authority.
- Where Ivory is purity and serenity, Gold is triumph — brilliant, confident, and commanding.
