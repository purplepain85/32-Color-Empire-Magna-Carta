# PEARL GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Luminous Stillness
### Color: Pearl (#F8F4E3)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Luminous Stillness  
Category: Grounded / Serene  
Description:
- Dragon resting in a calm, seated posture with wings folded softly against the body.
- Tail curled in a gentle loop around the front paws.
- Head slightly bowed, eyes half‑closed in meditative tranquility.
- Subtle halo‑like glow radiates from the chest and horns.
- Silhouette expresses purity, wisdom, and quiet inner power.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Pearl (#F8F4E3)
- Secondary Scales: Soft opalescent gradients shifting between rose, gold, and pale blue
- Belly Scales: Smooth ivory‑white with a satin sheen
- Wing Membranes: Translucent pearl‑sheen with iridescent highlights
- Horns & Claws: Polished ivory with faint rainbow refraction
- Eyes: Gentle warm‑white with a soft, steady glow

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Soft, diffuse lighting from all directions — no harsh shadows.
- Subtle iridescent reflections across scales and wings.
- Ambient glow around the dragon, like light through mist.
- Occasional drifting motes of light or dust illuminated by the aura.
- Lighting should feel sacred, calm, and weightless.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Misty sanctuary or quiet shoreline at dawn  
Foreground: Soft fog, floating motes, faint ripples of light  
Palette: Pearl‑white, soft gold, pale rose, gentle blue

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s twenty‑first spark.
- Embodies purity, clarity, and the quiet strength of stillness.
- The dragon of reflection, inner peace, and sacred beginnings.
- Where Garnet stands with fiery devotion, Pearl rests with luminous serenity — calm, pure, and timeless.
