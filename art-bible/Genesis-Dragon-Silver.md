# SILVER GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Moonlit Glide
### Color: Silver (#C0C0C0)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Moonlit Glide  
Category: Aerial  
Description:
- Dragon gliding smoothly across the sky with wings fully extended.
- Body level and streamlined, maintaining effortless altitude.
- Tail trailing behind in a gentle S-curve.
- Head angled slightly downward, observing the world below.
- Silhouette expresses elegance, calm motion, and lunar grace.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Silver (#C0C0C0)
- Secondary Scales: Cool metallic gradients along spine and limbs
- Belly Scales: Soft platinum with subtle reflective sheen
- Wing Membranes: Translucent silver with faint moonlit shimmer
- Horns & Claws: Polished chrome with sharp, clean edges
- Eyes: Pale icy-blue with a soft luminescent glow

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Cool moonlight from above and slightly behind.
- Soft highlights along wing edges and metallic scales.
- Subtle reflective glints on horns and claws.
- Ambient light from clouds or mist below.
- No warm tones — lighting must remain cold, serene, and nocturnal.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Night sky with drifting clouds illuminated by moonlight  
Foreground: Soft mist trails and faint sparkles of reflected light  
Palette: Silver, cool gray, pale blue, moonlit white

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s tenth spark.
- Embodies intuition, subtlety, and quiet mastery.
- The dragon of reflection, night travel, and silent observation.
- Where Gold is triumph and radiance, Silver is subtle power — calm, perceptive, and ethereal.
