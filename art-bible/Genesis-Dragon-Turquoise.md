## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Sky‑Ripple Drift  
Category: Aerial / Serpentine  
Description:
- Dragon drifting sideways through the air in a smooth, fluid S‑curve.
- Wings extended in a relaxed, gliding posture with soft curvature.
- Tail trailing behind in a long, ribbon‑like wave.
- Head angled slightly downward, expression calm and observant.
- Silhouette expresses fluidity, serenity, and effortless motion.

- Primary Scales: Turquoise (#40E0D0)

- Belly Scales: Pale aqua with soft reflective shimmer
- Wing Membranes: Translucent turquoise with faint ripple patterns

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Soft, cool daylight from above.
- Gentle highlights along wing edges and the flowing tail.
- Subtle reflective glints on scales, like light on water.
- Ambient light should feel airy, open, and sky‑washed.
- Occasional drifting mist or vapor catching turquoise light.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: High open sky with thin drifting clouds  
Foreground: Light vapor trails, soft wind‑shear effects  
Palette: Turquoise, aqua, teal, pale sky‑white

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s nineteenth spark.
- Embodies fluidity, adaptability, and emotional clarity.
- The dragon of open skies, shifting currents, and peaceful movement.
- Where Topaz rises with fiery hope, Turquoise drifts with calm freedom — airy, fluid, and unbound.
