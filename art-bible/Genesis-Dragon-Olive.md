## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Verdant Vigil  
Category: Grounded / Stoic  
Description:
- Dragon seated firmly on a moss‑covered stone, posture steady and unmoving.
- Wings folded in a broad, shield‑like formation along the sides.
- Tail wrapped around the base of the stone, tip resting calmly in front.
- Head angled slightly downward, eyes half‑open in quiet, watchful awareness.
- Silhouette evokes ancient forest guardians, patient and enduring.
