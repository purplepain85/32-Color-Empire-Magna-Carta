# AMBER GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Sun-Perch
### Color: Amber (#FFBF00)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Sun-Perch  
Category: Perched  
Description:
- Dragon perched on a tall rocky spire, side-profile.
- Wings partially open, catching warm sunlight.
- Tail draped downward in a relaxed, elegant curve.
- Head lifted slightly, eyes half-focused on the horizon.
- Silhouette expresses warmth, optimism, and radiant presence.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Amber (#FFBF00)
- Secondary Scales: Honey-gold gradients along spine and limbs
- Belly Scales: Soft warm gold with gentle reflective sheen
- Wing Membranes: Translucent amber with glowing edges
- Horns & Claws: Polished sandstone with subtle grain texture
- Eyes: Bright golden-yellow with a warm, inviting glow

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Strong warm sunlight from the upper left.
- Golden rim-light along wings, horns, and tail.
- Soft ambient bounce-light from the rocky perch.
- No harsh shadows — lighting should feel warm and uplifting.
- Occasional lens-flare style highlights on scales.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: High desert plateau at golden hour  
Foreground: Dust motes drifting in warm light  
Palette: Amber, honey-gold, warm sandstone, soft sky-blue

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s sixth spark.
- Embodies warmth, optimism, and radiant energy.
- The dragon of hope, renewal, and steady illumination.
- Where Emerald is stability, Amber is encouragement — bright, warm, and life-giving.
