### Pose: The Horizon Break

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Horizon Break  
Category: Aerial / Ascending  
Description:
- Dragon launching upward from a cliff edge, wings snapping open mid‑takeoff.
- Foreclaws pushing off the stone, hind legs extended behind.
- Tail whipping in a smooth upward arc, emphasizing lift.
- Head angled toward the rising sun, eyes bright and determined.
- Silhouette expresses optimism, ascent, and forward momentum.

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Strong sunrise lighting from the front‑right.
- Warm highlights along wings, horns, and chest.
- Soft golden bounce‑light from the cliff surface.
- No harsh shadows — lighting should feel hopeful and radiant.
- Occasional soft lens bloom around the brightest highlights.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Cliffside at sunrise with sweeping open sky  
Foreground: Dust motes drifting upward in warm light  
Palette: Topaz, honey‑gold, champagne, soft sky‑peach

- Represents the Empire’s eighteenth spark.
- Embodies optimism, renewal, and the courage to rise.
- The dragon of beginnings, dawn, and forward‑facing hope.
- Where Obsidian rises from darkness, Topaz rises toward light — warm, bright, and aspirational.
