### Pose: The Blood‑Oath Guardian
### Color: Maroon (#800000)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Blood‑Oath Guardian  
Category: Defensive / Resolute  
Description:
- Dragon standing in a wide, grounded stance, body angled protectively forward.
- Wings partially flared, forming a shield‑like barrier around its sides.
- Tail coiled tightly behind the legs, tip anchored like a stabilizing spike.
- Head lowered slightly, eyes locked forward with unwavering determination.
- Silhouette evokes ancient warriors, oath‑keepers, and guardians of sacred vows.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Maroon (#800000)
- Secondary Scales: Deep wine‑red and muted crimson gradients along spine and limbs
- Belly Scales: Dark rose‑brown with a matte, armored texture
- Wing Membranes: Semi‑opaque maroon with faint vein‑like patterns resembling old battle scars
- Horns & Claws: Dark iron‑black with subtle red reflections
- Eyes: Ember‑red with a steady, burning glow

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Warm, directional lighting from the front or side.
- Strong contrast to emphasize the dragon’s armored, battle‑worn silhouette.
- Occasional drifting dust or ash in the air.
- Highlights should feel heavy, grounded, and reminiscent of torchlight in a war‑hall.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Ancient battleground, stone courtyard, or oath‑hall  
Foreground: Dust, cracked stone, faint embers  
Palette: Maroon, wine red, iron black, muted gold
