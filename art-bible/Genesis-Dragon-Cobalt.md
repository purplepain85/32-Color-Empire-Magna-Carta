# COBALT GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Deep Current Surge
### Color: Cobalt (#0047AB)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Deep Current Surge  
Category: Aerial / Aquatic Hybrid  
Description:
- Dragon rising diagonally upward as if breaking through an invisible current.
- Wings angled sharply back, mimicking the hydrodynamic flow of fins.
- Tail long and fluid, trailing behind in a wave-like motion.
- Neck extended forward with a focused, predatory gaze.
- Silhouette expresses momentum, depth, and unstoppable forward force.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Cobalt (#0047AB)
- Secondary Scales: Deep navy gradients along spine and limbs
- Belly Scales: Cool steel-blue with subtle reflective sheen
- Wing Membranes: Translucent deep blue with faint ripple patterns
- Horns & Claws: Polished dark steel with blue reflections
- Eyes: Bright electric-blue with a piercing, aquatic intensity

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Cool, directional lighting from below (as if refracted through water).
- Soft caustic-like highlights dancing across scales.
- Strong rim-light along wing edges and tail.
- Mist or vapor trails catching blue light around the dragon.
- No warm tones — lighting must remain deep, cold, and fluid.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Stormy coastal sky or high-altitude mist with oceanic undertones  
Foreground: Water vapor trails, drifting mist, faint spray effects  
Palette: Cobalt, navy, steel-blue, cold white

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s thirteenth spark.
- Embodies depth, momentum, and relentless force.
- The dragon of tides, pressure, and the unseen power beneath the surface.
- Where Copper is coiled restraint, Cobalt is forward surge — deep, cold, and unstoppable.
