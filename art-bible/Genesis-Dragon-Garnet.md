### Pose: The Heartfire Stand

### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Heartfire Stand  
Category: Grounded / Regal  
Description:
- Dragon standing firmly with legs planted wide in a powerful stance.
- Wings half‑unfurled, forming a protective arc around the body.
- Chest pushed forward, glowing faintly with internal heat.
- Tail curled around one side like a guardian’s barrier.
- Head held high, eyes burning with deep emotional intensity.
- Silhouette expresses loyalty, passion, and unbreakable resolve.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Garnet (#8B0000)

- Belly Scales: Dark ruby with low, warm reflectivity
- Wing Membranes: Translucent blood‑red with subtle crystalline veins

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Warm, intimate lighting from the front‑left.
- Soft red highlights along chest, wings, and facial ridges.
- Subtle internal glow from the heart‑center of the chest.
- Shadows should feel heavy but comforting, like firelight.
- Occasional drifting ember motes around the dragon.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Ancient shrine or ceremonial stone circle  
Foreground: Ember dust, carved runes, soft warm haze  
Palette: Garnet, ruby, ember‑orange, deep shadow‑red

- Embodies devotion, emotional strength, and protective passion.
- The dragon of loyalty, oath‑keeping, and the fire that binds.
- Where Turquoise drifts with airy freedom, Garnet stands with rooted conviction — warm, fierce, and unwavering.
