# AMETHYST GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Astral Gaze
### Color: Amethyst (#9966CC)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Astral Gaze  
Category: Perched / Mystic  
Description:
- Dragon seated upright on a crystalline outcrop.
- Wings folded in a tall, cathedral-like arc behind the body.
- Tail wrapped loosely around the perch, tip glowing faintly.
- Head tilted slightly upward, eyes fixed on something beyond the visible world.
- Silhouette expresses mysticism, foresight, and cosmic awareness.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Amethyst (#9966CC)
- Secondary Scales: Deep violet gradients along spine and wing bones
- Belly Scales: Soft lavender with subtle iridescent shimmer
- Wing Membranes: Translucent violet with faint star-like speckles
- Horns & Claws: Polished dark crystal with purple refractive edges
- Eyes: Bright white-violet with a slow, pulsing glow

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Cool, ethereal lighting from above and behind.
- Soft purple highlights along horns, wings, and facial ridges.
- Subtle internal glow from chest and tail tip.
- Ambient light should feel cosmic, diffuse, and otherworldly.
- Occasional tiny sparkles or motes drifting around the dragon.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Crystal plateau under a star-filled twilight sky  
Foreground: Floating dust motes, faint magical haze  
Palette: Violet, lavender, deep indigo, soft white

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s sixteenth spark.
- Embodies intuition, magic, and the unseen forces of the cosmos.
- The dragon of prophecy, arcane knowledge, and astral perception.
- Where Vermilion is explosive action, Amethyst is cosmic stillness — mystical, calm, and infinitely aware.
