# MINT GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Fresh Awakening
### Color: Mint (#98FFCC)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Fresh Awakening  
Category: Light / Uplifted  
Description:
- Dragon rising from a crouched position with a gentle, refreshing upward motion.
- Wings unfurl in a soft, breezy arc, like leaves opening to morning light.
- Tail curves in a loose, airy loop behind the body.
- Head lifted slightly, expression bright and alert, as if sensing a new beginning.
- Silhouette evokes renewal, clarity, and the first breath of spring.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Mint (#98FFCC)
- Secondary Scales: Soft gradients of pale aqua and cool green along spine and limbs
- Belly Scales: Frosted white‑mint with a smooth satin finish
- Wing Membranes: Translucent mint‑green with faint leaf‑vein patterns
- Horns & Claws: Polished pale jade with subtle reflective highlights
- Eyes: Bright spring‑green with a lively, sparkling glow

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Bright, diffused lighting with a cool green tint.
- Soft highlights that mimic morning dew or fresh leaves.
- Occasional drifting particles resembling pollen or tiny motes of light.
- Overall lighting should feel refreshing, clean, and invigorating.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Spring glade, dew‑lit meadow, or fresh forest clearing  
Foreground: Soft grass, drifting pollen, faint morning mist  
Palette: Mint, pale aqua, soft green, white‑gold

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s thirty‑first spark.
- Embodies renewal, clarity, and the refreshing energy of new beginnings.
- The dragon of spring, breath, and the gentle return of life.
- Where Maroon stands with fierce devotion, Mint rises with gentle rejuvenation — light, hopeful, and quietly revitalizing.
