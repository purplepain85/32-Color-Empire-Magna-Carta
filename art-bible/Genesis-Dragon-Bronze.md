# BRONZE GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Forge-Back Stride
### Color: Bronze (#CD7F32)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Forge-Back Stride  
Category: Grounded  
Description:
- Dragon walking forward with slow, deliberate power.
- Wings folded tightly against the body, emphasizing muscular form.
- Tail sweeping low behind, heavy and controlled.
- Head angled slightly downward, eyes focused ahead with determination.
- Silhouette expresses craftsmanship, endurance, and forged strength.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Bronze (#CD7F32)
- Secondary Scales: Burnt copper gradients along spine and limbs
- Belly Scales: Matte dark-bronze with low reflectivity
- Wing Membranes: Deep metallic brown with subtle hammered texture
- Horns & Claws: Aged copper with patina hints at the edges
- Eyes: Warm molten-orange with a steady, smoldering glow

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Warm, directional lighting from a low angle (forge-like).
- Strong highlights on metallic scales, especially shoulders and spine.
- Soft orange bounce-light from imagined molten surfaces.
- Minimal ambient light — shadows should feel heavy and industrial.
- Occasional sparks or ember reflections on scales.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Ancient forge hall or volcanic workshop floor  
Foreground: Scattered embers, glowing cracks in stone  
Palette: Bronze, copper, dark brown, ember-orange

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s eleventh spark.
- Embodies craftsmanship, endurance, and the will to build.
- The dragon of creation, labor, and the shaping of raw potential.
- Where Silver is lunar grace, Bronze is industry — grounded, strong, and forged by effort.
