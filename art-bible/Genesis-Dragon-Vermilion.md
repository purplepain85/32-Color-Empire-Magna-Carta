# VERMILION GENESIS DRAGON
### Pose: The Flame-Lunge
### Color: Vermilion (#E34234)
### Chain: Solana

## I. SILHOUETTE & POSE
Pose Name: The Flame-Lunge  
Category: Grounded / Dynamic  
Description:
- Dragon lunging forward with one foreclaw extended.
- Wings flared outward in a sharp, aggressive arc.
- Tail whipping behind in a fiery curve.
- Neck thrust forward, jaws slightly open as if releasing heat.
- Silhouette expresses aggression, speed, and explosive intent.

## II. COLOR TREATMENT
- Primary Scales: Vermilion (#E34234)
- Secondary Scales: Hot crimson-orange gradients along spine and limbs
- Belly Scales: Pale molten-orange with subtle internal glow
- Wing Membranes: Translucent vermilion with heated edge-lighting
- Horns & Claws: Dark obsidian with ember-like cracks
- Eyes: Bright gold-orange with a fierce, burning intensity

## III. LIGHTING RULES
- Strong frontal lighting to emphasize the forward lunge.
- Hot highlights along claws, horns, and wing edges.
- Subtle heat distortion around the dragon’s mouth and chest.
- Shadows stretched backward to enhance motion.
- No cool tones — lighting must remain fiery, aggressive, and kinetic.

## IV. BACKGROUND
Environment: Scorched battlefield or cracked volcanic plain  
Foreground: Flying embers, dust trails, heat shimmer  
Palette: Vermilion, ember-orange, charcoal, molten gold

## V. SYMBOLISM & LORE
- Represents the Empire’s fifteenth spark.
- Embodies aggression, decisive action, and fiery momentum.
- The dragon of battle, confrontation, and raw emotional force.
- Where Jade is patient and coiled, Vermilion is explosive — hot, immediate, and unstoppable.
